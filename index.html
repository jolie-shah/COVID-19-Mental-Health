Introduction

Along with the COVID-19 pandemic that has infected tens of millions of people throughout the world, the abrupt shift in lifestyles from mass lockdowns has brought the attention of another concerning issue: a mental health crisis. Prior to the pandemic, studies have shown a distinctly surging trend of serious psychological distress among adolescents and young adults. This conclusion would infer the younger generation to be highly vulnerable to the mental health challenges that have emerged by the COVID-19 lockdowns.

Lockdowns has been determinetal to mental health as a whole. The social-isolation from the world has made people suffer from high levels of anxiety, depression, substance abuse, and disordered eating, especially students. Students in particular had their educational experience change drastically. They have become increasingly uncertain of their education due to the large disadvantages of distance learning and the outcome of their future careers due to the lack of opprotunities. In the United States, higher education are more costly than other countries, raising financial concerns when dealing with student debt. Along with the lockdowns, the loss of family and friends from deadly disease as well as facing the severe symptoms themselves has instilled more fear and distress.

The mental health crisis has already taken a devastating toll on the younger generations, and with the psycholgical influences of COVID-19, it is only getting worse.

The Question

How has the psychological impact of the COVID-19 pandemic increased rates of mental illnesses and psychological distress among students?

The Hypothesis

The surging rates of mental ilnesses and psycholgical distress among students has been a major issue. With the social consequences of COVID-19, this has further amplified the situation. If a student is facing any of these negative social consequenecs, then the distress of the situation would damage their mental health. The more serious COVID-19 cases in the region, the more the younger generations will display this effect.
